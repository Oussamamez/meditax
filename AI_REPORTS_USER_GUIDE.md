# AI-Generated Financial Reports - User Guide

## Quick Start

### Access the Feature
1. Log in to your MediTax Connect account as a healthcare professional
2. Click on "AI Reports" in the navigation menu
3. You'll see the AI Financial Reports dashboard

### Dashboard Overview

The dashboard displays:
- **Statistics Cards**: Overview of your reports
  - Total Reports generated
  - Approved reports
  - Draft reports in progress
  - When the last report was generated

- **Report List**: All your generated reports organized by year
  - Filter by year using the year selector
  - View reports by status (All, Approved, Drafts)

## Generating Reports

### Creating a New Report

1. Click the **"Generate New Report"** button (top right)
2. In the modal dialog, select:
   - **Report Type**: Choose the type of analysis you need
     - **Comprehensive Analysis**: Full financial overview
     - **Tax Summary**: Focus on tax implications
     - **Expense Analysis**: Detailed expense breakdown
     - **Growth Analysis**: Year-over-year comparison
     - **Custom Report**: Create your own analysis
   - **Year**: Select the fiscal year to analyze
3. Click **"Generate Report"**
4. The report will be generated automatically (usually within seconds)

### What Gets Analyzed

Your report includes analysis of:
- **Revenue**: Total income for the selected year
- **Expenses**: Operating costs and deductions
- **Profit**: Net income after expenses
- **Taxes**: Estimated tax liability and effective tax rates
- **Documents**: References to your uploaded documents
- **Trends**: Comparison with previous years

## Working with Reports

### View a Report

1. Find the report in the list
2. Click the **Eye Icon** to view details
3. The report viewer shows:
   - Executive summary
   - Detailed financial analysis
   - AI-generated recommendations
   - Key financial metrics
   - Charts and visualizations

### Report Status

Reports have different statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🟡 Draft | Report has been generated but not yet reviewed |
| 🔵 Generated | Report is complete and ready for review |
| 🟣 Reviewed | Accountant has reviewed the report |
| 🟢 Approved | Report is officially approved |
| ⚫ Archived | Older report for reference |

### Download Report

1. Click the **Download Icon** for a report
2. Report will be saved as PDF to your computer
3. Share with your accountant or accountant firm

### Delete Report

1. Click the **Trash Icon** to delete a report
2. Confirm the deletion when prompted
3. **Note**: This action cannot be undone

## Key Metrics Explained

### Profit Margin (%)
- Shows what percentage of your revenue you keep as profit
- Higher is better
- Formula: (Revenue - Expenses) / Revenue × 100

**Example**: 40% profit margin means you keep $40 for every $100 earned

### Income-to-Expense Ratio
- Shows how many times your expenses are covered by income
- Ratio of 2.0 means you earn $2 for every $1 spent
- Higher is better

### Effective Tax Rate (%)
- Shows the percentage of your profit that goes to taxes
- Based on estimated income tax calculations
- Varies by location and business structure

### YoY Growth (%)
- Year-over-year growth comparison
- Positive (+) means growth compared to previous year
- Negative (-) means decline

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How often can I generate reports?
**A**: You can generate reports as often as needed. There's no limit on the number of reports you can create.

### Q: Can my accountant see my reports?
**A**: Yes! You can share your reports with your accountant. They can access them if you grant permission, and they receive copies when you mark reports for review.

### Q: Are my financial details secure?
**A**: Yes. All data is encrypted and protected by the same security measures as the rest of MediTax Connect. Only you, your accountant, and admins can view your reports.

### Q: Can I edit a report after generation?
**A**: The AI-generated content is fixed, but you can add notes and update the status. For changes to financial data, update your records and generate a new report.

### Q: What if I disagree with the analysis?
**A**: Discuss the report with your accountant. They can provide professional guidance on the recommendations. You can also generate a custom report with different parameters.

### Q: How accurate are the recommendations?
**A**: Recommendations are AI-generated based on your financial data. They should be reviewed by a professional accountant before implementing.

### Q: Can I print reports?
**A**: Yes! Use the browser print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) to print any report. PDF downloads are coming soon.

### Q: What data does AI use for analysis?
**A**: 
- Your income and expenses
- Number of uploaded documents
- Your business type
- Tax information
- Previous year comparisons

## Troubleshooting

### Report Won't Generate
1. Verify you have financial records for that year
2. Ensure all required fields are filled in
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try refreshing the page

### Can't View Report Details
1. Check that JavaScript is enabled in your browser
2. Clear browser cache and refresh
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection

### Numbers Don't Match My Records
1. Verify financial data in Dashboard
2. Check that all documents are uploaded
3. Generate a new report after updating data
4. Discuss with your accountant

### Permission Denied Error
1. Ensure you're logged in
2. Check that you're a healthcare professional
3. Verify your account hasn't been suspended
4. Try logging out and back in
